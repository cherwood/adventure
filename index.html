How it works

Based on the method «Don’t Break the Chain», you get to decide an activity you’d like to do everyday, and the more days in a row you keep up with this habit, the more you get rewarded with XP points and freaking (virtual) cash.

Your goal here is to never break the chain.

  • 1Push me everyday

    Each day you achieve one of your predefined tasks, push the red button and feel immediate joy.

  • 2Harder, better, cooler

    Gradually incorporate good habits into your lifestyle; gain money and stylise your character the way you want !

  • 3Happiness (KINDA)

    Soon, become (virtually) rich and brag to your friends how good your in-game character looks.

Easy as hell

Simply choose your character, give them a name, set-up your habits and you’re pretty much good to go.
